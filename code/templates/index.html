HOW IT WORKS
Ferry uses the infrastructure of the human network to allow international delivery to underserved markets.
Match
In some parts of the world it's impossible to buy anything online. We match you with someone headed your way to deliver select items from Amazon with more retailers soon to come.
Fly
Ferry connects travellers to folks in countries that are underserved by traditional ecommerce companies and shipping networks. Every traveller and every package is verified and insured.
Meet
Meet your match at a pre-determined location to receive your package. Our goal is to provide end-to-end delivery services by the end of 2017.
